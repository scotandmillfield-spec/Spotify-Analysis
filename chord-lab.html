Hit Generate to spin up a set.
MIDI files contain block-chord voicings (one bar per chord) at your chosen tempo. Bass sets how the low note moves: Smooth places inversions for a walking bassline, Root keeps it on each chord's root, Pedal holds a tonic drone underneath the shifting chords — that hypnotic deep-progressive staple. Preview is a simple built-in pad; your DAW instruments will sound different. Nothing is uploaded; everything runs in your browser.
